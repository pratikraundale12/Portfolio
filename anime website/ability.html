Top 10 Abilities in Jujutsu Kaissen

10

Cursed Speech

Toge Inumaki

Technique Debut: Episode 8 (Boring)

Inumaki is easily one of the most fascinating characters in Jujutsu Kaisen. The fact that he can only use a limited vocabulary to convey his points highlights the fact that his Cursed Technique is incredibly powerful perhaps more than he can control himself.

His Cursed Speech allows Inumaki to control the actions of his enemies by shouting orders that are powered by his Cursed Energy. Such a powerful technique takes a huge toll on his body, with Inumaki being rendered unable to speak if he ends up overusing this technique.

9

Womb Profusion

Kenjaku

Technique Debut: Chapter 205 (Stars and Oil)

Kenjaku is a heinous curse user who has remained alive for many years with an ability that lets him transplant his brain into others, rendering him immortal until the brain itself is taken out. Not only does this curse user take on the abilities of its possessed body, but Kenjaku can also use Domain Expansion to completely decimate his enemies if he wishes.

Womb Profusion, his Domain Expansion, is a disgusting technique to witness firsthand. He forms a pillar made entirely out of cursed spirits that can hit enemies for massive damage, essentially killing any unprepared foe who has no idea just how powerful this Domain Expansion technique really is. The fact that the true power of this technique hasn't been properly seen yet is a shame.

8

Ten Shadows

Megumi Fushiguro

Technique Debut: Episode 1 (Ryomen Sukuna)

Megumi is poised to become one of the most unique and engaging characters in Jujutsu Kaisen. His Ten Shadows power might be fairly weak right now, but its sheer potential is limitless. It's going to take Megumi quite some time to master this technique, but there's no denying that watching him use the Ten Shadows technique at its maximum potential is going to be an absolute blast.

If Megumi manages to hone his ability to a T, there's no telling what this man will be capable of. However, he has been on the back foot in most of his battles, which does take away a bit from the power of his technique.

7

Disaster Flames

Jogo

Technique Debut: Episode 6 (After the Rain)

Jujutsu Kaisen 0 is an amazing movie that is filled to the brim with some great animation along with a fun story that focuses on a special individual called Yuta Okkotsu, a man who believes that he's haunted by the spirit of his dead lover, only for it to be revealed that he was the person who ended up cursing Rika instead. This realization allows him to finally control his Cursed Energy and become one of the most powerful Jujutsu Sorcerers in the entire series.

5

Comedian

Fumihiko Takaba

Technique Debut: Episode 168 (Tokyo No. 1 Colony, Part 8)s

It takes something really special for a relatively new jujutsu sorcerer to showcase an ability so powerful that Gojo himself acknowledges just how dangerous this power really is. Fumihiko is a character introduced during the Culling Game who has the power to turn his jokes into reality, which means that the sky's the limit for this powerful Cursed Technique.

The only respite that most of his opponents have is that Fumihiko himself is unaware of just how powerful this ability is. There are times when he can lose faith in his own jokes, which is understandable given his status as a failed comedian. Still, it's oddly unnerving to see a character who has so much power that they themselves don't know how to control it, making it easy to see why fighting this sorcerer can be pretty challenging indeed.

4

Spiderweb

Ryomen Sukuna

Technique Debut: Chapter 215 (Fearsome Womb, Part 7)

It goes to say that Sukuna has some of the most powerful abilities in the history of the series. There's a reason why even the toughest Jujutsu Sorcerers fear his presence since this Malevolent Being can level entire buildings and tear apart any enemy that may be unlucky enough to stand in his way.

One of his abilities that essentially magnifies his Cleave technique to his surroundings is Spiderweb, leaving a huge trail of destruction in its wake. Any person unlucky to be caught in this attack will be sliced into ribbons in no time at all, which can make for a rather unceremonious demise indeed.

3

Hollow Technique: Purple

Gojo Satoru

Technique Debut: Episode 20 (Nonstandard)

Combining both the Red and Blue powers of Gojo's Limitless technique makes for a brilliant spectacle of explosive power. Gojo wastes no time in bringing out this move during the Goodwill Event when Hanami starts wreaking havoc.

This technique is so powerful that Hanami would've been completely obliterated had it not been for his swift thinking. The true potential of this technique is yet to be seen, and fans can't wait for Gojo to whip out this technique once again in a later season of Jujutsu Kaisen.

2

Malevolent Shrine

Ryomen Sukuna

Technique Debut: Episode 4 (Fearsome Womb)

Sukuna is one of the most powerful Cursed Spirits in the history of Jujutsu Kaisen. So, it would only make sense that his Domain would be breathtaking to behold and just as deadly.

Watching Sukuna sitting on top of his throne without a care in the world makes for an incredibly intimidating scene every single time viewers have the privilege of peering into this Domain. One can only wait and see the true power of this Domain in action when Sukuna gets invested in a battle.
